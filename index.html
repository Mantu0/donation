Our Services

There are several services available to support orphaned individuals. Many governments and nonprofit organizations provide financial aid, housing, and healthcare services to help orphans. Child welfare programs offer emotional counseling, mentorship, and educational support, ensuring they have access to schools and vocational training. Additionally, legal assistance is often provided to secure guardianship or adoption. Orphanages, both local and international, may offer safe shelter and resources for social integration. Some religious and community organizations also provide food, clothing, and social services.
